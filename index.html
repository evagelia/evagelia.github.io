About

Hello! I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow at the ARC at Georgia Tech . I mainly work with Sahil Singla, Lauren Steimle and Gian Garcia. I'm broadly interested in the theoretical aspects of designing algorithms under uncertainty, approximation and online algorithms and machine learning. I'm also interested in applying these theoretical tools to more applied settings, currently working on maternal healthcare problems.

Before joining GaTech I did my PhD at the CS Department of University of Wisconsin-Madison where I was fortunate to be advised by Christos Tzamos, and before joining UW-Madison I did my undergrad in Greece, in the ECE Department of National Technical University of Athens.
